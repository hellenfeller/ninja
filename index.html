Ninja Biography

Tyler Blevins (born Richard Tyler Blevins), or more commonly known as “Ninja” is an internet personality famous for his fortnite live streams. His explosion of popularity throughout 2018 has helped him become the most followed streamer on twitch.tv (over 14 million followers as of may 2019).

Ninja hosting a streaming event at Time Square during New Years 2018



Ninja has gained alot of his recognition from playing the popular game fortnite. He streams the game on the platform Twitch.Tv along side many other streamers.







Ninja was featured on the cover of an espn magazine.
