Early Life

Ninja was born June 5th, 1991. He was from Detroit, but moved to Chicago when he was only a year old. His youth life consisted of playing video games and playing sports in the suburbs of Chicago. In high school he was an avid video game player and was a soccer player as well.

Ninja long before his streaming career


Ultimately, young Ninja made a decision after graduating that was to change the rest of his life. Pursuing the career of Professional gaming was what Ninja decided to do. He started off with Halo 3 in 2009, and competed under multiple well known teams in the online gaming community. Some teams that Ninja has been recognized for included Cloud9, Team Liquid, and currently is playing under Luminosity Gaming. In 2011, Ninja started his online streaming of video games career.
